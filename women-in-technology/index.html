Women in Technology Conference

Guild of Students
University of Birmingham
Saturday 6th February, 2016
10:30 - 17:00

A day of Industry Speakers visiting UoB

UoB's Women In Science and Engineering and Computer Science Societies have worked together to host a conference of whose speakers are women working in Technology.

Everyone is invited to attend a day of talks, workshops, and networking sessions from invited industry speakers and event sponsors.

Lunch and refreshments will be provided thanks to the support of our sponsors, listed below, who will also be in attendance.
